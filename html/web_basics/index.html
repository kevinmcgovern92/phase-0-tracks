Game of Thrones Spoilers Ahead

As an avid GoT fan, and reader of A Song of Ice and Fire, Season 6 has been full of surprises and delight so far. On this page, I will go through some theories and whether they've been borne out by the show, proven false, or have not been addressed yet.

Things I got right

  1. Melissandre brings Jon back to life with the Red Kiss
  2. Jon leaves the Night's Watch saying "My watch is ended"
  3. Ned and Howland Reed are the only people who know the truth of R+L=J
  4. Ser Robert Strong is Gregor Clegane brought back by Qyburn's necromancy
  5. The High Sparrow is a true fanatic and not a sycophant or imposter
  6. Euron killed Balon

Things I got wrong (or never saw coming)

  • Sand Snakes overthrow Prince Doran Martell, take control of Dorne via coup
  • Ramsay murders Roose Bolton
  • Arya doesn't cheat her way through the Kindly Man's training
  • Daenerys gets taken back to Vaes Dothrak
  • Theon and Sansa get away from Winterfell alive
  • The Umbers found Rickon and turn him over to Ramsay
